## How to use
FTP into your PlayStation 5 and delete, rename or backup the `cheats` folder from `/data/etaHEN/` and replace it with the `cheats` folder from my collection.

`IMPORTANT!` **Restart and re-jb the PlayStation. Enjoy your brand new cheats**.

## Find compatible cheats
Go [**HERE TO OPEN THE CHEATS PAGE**](https://raw.githack.com/TeeKay87/HEN-Cheats-Collection/master/list/index.html) where you can find all available cheats. Search for either an `ID`, `title` or `cheat creator` to to filter the list.

Play your way.
